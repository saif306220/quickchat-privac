QuickChat Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 26 November 2025

1. Introduction

QuickChat respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the QuickChat app.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • QuickChat does NOT store contacts, messages, or phone numbers.
  • No login or registration is required.
  • We do not collect personal information such as name, email, or phone number.
  • We do not track your location.

3. Analytics

We may use anonymous analytics to improve app performance. These analytics do not include personal information and cannot identify you.

4. Security

QuickChat ensures that your data stays private because the app does not store any user data.

5. Third-Party Links

QuickChat may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites. Please review their privacy policies separately.

6. Children's Privacy

QuickChat is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13 years old.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page with the new effective date.
